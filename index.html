Q> HOW TO PLAY THIS GAME ?
STEP 1> CHOOSE A COLOR FROM THE TOP BOX (BOX IDENTIFICATION - THERE IS NO YES & NO button THERE)
STEP 2> CLICK YES JUST ONCE IF THE COLOR IS AVALIABLE ON OTHER BOXES AND NO IF THE COLOR IS NOT THERE
STEP 3> CLICK REVEAL THEN AUTOMATICALLY THE COLOR WILL BE SHOWN WHICH YOU HAVE CHOOSEN AND TO PLAY AGAING CLICK RESTART

NOTICE * > BE CAREFUL WHILE CLICKNG YES BUTTON MAKE SURE YOU ARE CLICKNG ONLY ONCE TO THE YES BUTTON.. IF CLICK MORE THAN ONCE THEN NO COLOR WILL BE REVEALED OR SOME OTHER COLOR WILL BE APPEARED AFTER YOU CLICK REVEAL BUTTON...
